About UNJobs

Connecting talent with purpose since 1999. We are the original and most comprehensive job source for development practitioners, humanitarian workers, and international civil servants.

Our Mission

UNJobs is dedicated to connecting talented professionals with meaningful careers at leading international organizations worldwide. We believe that the right people in the right positions can create lasting positive change in our world.

Since our founding in 1999, we have been the trusted bridge between ambitious professionals seeking purpose-driven careers and international organizations working to address the world's most pressing challenges.

Our Story
