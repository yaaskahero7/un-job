Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I create an account?

Click on "Sign In" in the top navigation, then select "Create Account". Fill in your details and verify your email address to get started.

Is UNJobs free to use?

Yes, UNJobs is completely free for job seekers. You can search, browse, and apply to jobs without any cost.

How often are new jobs posted?

New jobs are posted daily. We update our database in real-time as organizations submit new opportunities.

Can I set up job alerts?

Yes, you can create personalized job alerts based on your preferences for location, organization, and job category.

How do I apply for jobs?

Each job listing includes application instructions. Most applications are submitted directly to the hiring organization through their official channels.

Do you offer career counseling?

Yes, we provide career guidance resources and can connect you with experienced professionals for personalized advice.
